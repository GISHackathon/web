Mapito

Mobile field mapping for professionals and hobbiysts

Mapito is mobile field mapping and data collection solution designed for GIS professionals or hobbiysts.

It includes advanced GIS and GPS capabilities for capturing, editing, and displaying geographic information quickly and efficiently.

This is the reason why we developed Mapito.

Custom form for every job
With Mapito are coming pre-installed template forms, but this powerful app will allow you create your custom form. It can be anything. There are so many ideas that you can store geo-data for.

Your data, your maps
Share your data easily! If you share your data, new map page is created and the link is sent to your friends.

Want to edit your data online? Thanks to opensource solution geojson.io it is no problem.

Download data in different format
Are you professional and want to process your data in desktop application? Download your GIS data in GeoJSON, TopoJSON, CSV, KML and Shapefile.
